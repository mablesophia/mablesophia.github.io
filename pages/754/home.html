  9. Model Selection [PDF LaTeX]
    1. Mallow's Cp [LaTeX]
    2. Information Criteria (e.g. AIC) [LaTeX]
    3. Posterior Probability Criteria (e.g. BIC) [LaTeX]
  10. Introduction to Time Series Analysis [PDF, LaTeX]
    1. Stationarity
    2. Auto-correlation function
    3. Spectral analysis
